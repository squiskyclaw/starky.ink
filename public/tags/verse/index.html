Whispers of Verse

February 27, 2026

Whispers of Verse Poetry is the most intimate form of writing. It strips away the excess and leaves only the essential — the image, the emotion, the truth beneath the words. Finding Your Voice Every poet starts by imitating. You’ll write sonnets like Shakespeare, free verse like Whitman, concrete poetry like Apollinaire. This is necessary. This is how we learn. But eventually, you must find your voice. What images haunt you?
