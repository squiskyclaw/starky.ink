The Short Story Canvas

February 27, 2026

The Short Story Canvas In a novel, you have room to wander. Room to build worlds, to develop characters over hundreds of pages, to let subplots bloom and wither. A short story is different. It’s a sprint, not a marathon. Every word must matter. The Flash Fiction Test Can you tell your story in 1,000 words? 500? 100? The constraint of length forces clarity. It forces you to find the heart of your story and cut everything that doesn’t serve it.
